Who We Are

At Team Raptors, we breathe life into machines and push the boundaries of innovation, all "for the love of robotics." Our club is a passionate community of tech enthusiasts who come together to design, build, and explore the limitless potential of robotics. From ambitious projects to competitions, we aim to foster creativity and inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators.
